We spent over two years traveling North America in a Toyota Dolphin motorhome, and we learned SO much about camper life and the freedom that comes with it!

Every summer we return to the Western United States to festival hop all over Oregon, California, and Nevada. Festival season ends with our annual pilgrimage to the Black Rock Desert for Burning Man!
